How I Work

My analytics process is rooted in human-centered discovery, design, and enablement — the same framework applied to each assignment.

Discover

Start with stakeholder language. Clarify success metrics, constraints, and how the insight will be consumed.

Design

Prototype data stories with storyboards, low-fi dashboards, and narrative outlines that align expectations.

Develop

Build reliable data pipelines and analytical layers with Python, SQL, and Plotly/Altair for reproducibility.

Deliver

Ship actionable artifacts, document assumptions, and coach teams on iterating insights into decisions.
